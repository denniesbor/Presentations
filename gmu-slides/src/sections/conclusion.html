Summary of Contributions

  1. End-to-end coupling from solar forcing to economy-wide impact with preserved uncertainty
  2. Multi-system coverage spanning terrestrial infrastructure and space-based infrastructure
  3. Operator-facing and policy-facing metrics (transformer failure probability, propellant consumption, affected customers, daily GDP impact)
  4. Cross-hazard comparative assessment positioning space weather alongside familiar hazards
  5. Open-source tooling with documented workflows for validation and broader adoption
